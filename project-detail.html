Full container load movement of industrial equipment from the Port of Aqaba to Rotterdam. Three 40ft HC containers across multiple vessels, with end-to-end port operations management and full customs brokerage on both ends.

Project Overview

The client required movement of heavy industrial machinery destined for a manufacturing facility in the Netherlands. The cargo required specialized container configuration, hazardous goods documentation, and coordination with multiple port authorities across the route. Our team managed the entire operation from container booking through to final delivery, including transshipment coordination at Port Said.

warning The Challenge

Coordinating hazardous goods classification across 3 port authorities with different documentation standards while meeting a fixed unloading window in Rotterdam that couldn't be moved.

check_circle The Solution

Pre-cleared all hazmat paperwork 10 days ahead. Negotiated transshipment slot at Port Said to align with the Rotterdam window. Dedicated ops contact on-ground at Aqaba for day-of loading supervision.
