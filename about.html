Our Story

Built on Trust.
Driven by Precision.

Reflection Freight Forwarding and Logistic Services is a Jordan-based logistics company headquartered in Amman, providing reliable international freight and supply chain solutions.

We specialize in moving cargo efficiently across borders through air freight, sea freight, and land transport — handling the complexity of logistics so our clients can focus on their business.

From small shipments to full container loads, our team ensures every shipment is handled with precision, reliability, and transparency.

Our Mission

Simplify Global Logistics

To deliver reliable freight solutions built on precision, trust, and operational excellence — helping businesses move goods faster, safer, and more efficiently across international markets.

Our Vision

Most Trusted in the Middle East

To become one of the most trusted logistics partners in the Middle East, connecting regional businesses to global trade networks through innovative and dependable supply chain solutions.
